Story

The first prototype of IDbox was built during a London Blockchain Week Hackathon in January 2017. It was based on the needs of Papua New Guinea, a Pacific country with low internet access, limited electricity and limited smart phone penetration. IDbox project - which was originally named PapuanyBox - won the competition and is now being developed for use case trials in Papua New Guinea and elsewhere.
