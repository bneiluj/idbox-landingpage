"1.5 Billion People Around the World Cannot Prove Their Identity".

Secured id

The identity data is held immutably on the Blockchain. The identity data remains in the ownership of the individual and individuals provide permissions as to who can see what data for which purpose and for how long. Blockchain records can be used to establish an identity where no state identity exists or can be used.

No smartphone

IDbox uses basic analogue phones as 80% of people in developing countries don't have smartphones. This makes the service easily accessible.

Potentials

IDbox has the potential to be used in humanitarian emergencies, in remote poorly serviced populations and can also ensure that cash transfers and donor funds reach the intended beneficiaries.
