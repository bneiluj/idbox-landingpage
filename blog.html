August 2017

USA Tour and pilot in Papua New Guinea

USA Tour

A presentation of IDbox was given in 4 different cities of United State of America: Washington DC, Chicago, San Francisco and New York City. All of them received a big round of applause from the audience and a lot of traction from different businesses and worldwide institutions.

IDbox pilot in Papua New Guinea

Julien Bouteloup is going to Papua New Guinea for 3 weeks to demonstrate the latest version of IDbox and run a pilot in Lalaura village which is 6 hours drive south of Port Moresby. The plan is to register people from the village through the IDbox device for them to access Heathcare, Voting, Remittance and Electricy. You can be part of the journey by following IDbox's twitter or the Founder's twitter account.

July

Europe tour and finalizing IDbox project.

Europe

Idbox was presented to different conferences in Europe and was always the most appreciated project.

Technology

A new version of IDbox has been developed over the past 4 month to extend the potential of the project. This new IDbox is called IDbox Plus named IDbox Plus v1.0. After enrolling new user through the IDbox Plus, the user or the business can directly have access to Heathcare registration, voting system, money transfers and also "Pay As You Go" for Electricity.

June 2017

Three more pilots

Pilots

After traveling to different conferences and places in the world - IDbox has received tremendous traction with different institutions, businesses and worldwide organisations. The plan is to run one pilot in Southern Pacific, one in South America, one in Africa and one in Indonesia before the end of the year.

Avril & Mai 2017

Extended idea

IDbox Plus

IDbox core idea is to build a cost efficient device that enables people to create a unique identity in developing countries. IDbox Plus is an extended device to show the vast number of possibilities when a person creates a unique identity. It opens the door to healthcare registration, polls, remittance, pay as you go for electricity, etc.
